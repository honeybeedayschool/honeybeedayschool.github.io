Our Approach

Our approach is simple. We focus on helping your children grow as individuals in an environment where they feel safe, happy, and cared for. We know that early childhood development is crucial for long-term growth. Research has shown that "from the time of conception to the first day of kindergarten, development proceeds at a pace exceeding that of any subsequent stage of life."

Social Development

One of our main goals is to help your children develop socially as individuals. The reason we focus so strongly on social development is because these are some of the first years your child will be spending away from home and around other children. In fact, research has shown that strong social and emotional foundations built during the stages of early childhood development have a strong influence on a child's behavior, academic performance, career, and health!

Fun & Nurturing Environment

Since these are some of the first years your child will be spending away from home and around new people, it's important your child feels safe and cared for. The way a child's environment is set-up impacts how that child feels, acts, develops, and behaves, according to research. Our teachers have great experience working with children and have designed and cultivated an environment where your child will not only be safe, but will be able to meet other children, pick up new skills, and "bee" the best they can "bee!"

Educational Foundation

Our teachers have been nurturing and teaching young children for years. HoneyBee Day School follows a Montessori method of education. Our teachings include self-directed activities, hands-on learning, creativity, and collaboration. Research has shown that early childhood education programs are linked to higher education levels and higher academic performance in the future.
